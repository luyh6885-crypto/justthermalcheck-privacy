Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

Summary: JustThermalCheck has no user accounts, advertising, or analytics. The developer does not operate a server that collects your print content or personal data. App data is processed and stored on your device unless you choose to send it to a printer, another device, the Android share sheet, or Google Play for a purchase.

1. Information processed by the app

To provide printing and device-management features, the app may process the following information locally on your device:

  • Printer and connection information, such as device name, Bluetooth address, IP address, port, and connection preferences.
  • Content you create or select for printing, including text, commands, templates, barcodes, QR codes, and images.
  • App settings, print tasks, history, logs, quick parameters, and backup data.

This information is not uploaded to a server operated by the developer.

2. Permissions and their purposes

  • Bluetooth and nearby devices: used to discover, connect to, and communicate with Bluetooth printers.
  • Location: on Android versions that require it, location permission is requested only to scan for nearby Bluetooth devices. The app does not track, store, or transmit your location.
  • Camera and photos: used only when you choose to take or select an image for printing. Images are processed on your device and sent only to the printer or destination you select.
  • Network and Wi-Fi state: used for local-network printer connections, TCP server features, and device-to-device data transfer. The app does not use these features to upload your print content to the developer.

3. Google Play purchases

Optional Pro purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. Google may process your Google account, payment, order, and device information under the Google Privacy Policy. JustThermalCheck checks purchase status and receives a purchase token/signature needed to unlock features, but it does not receive or store your full payment-card details.

4. Sharing and disclosure

The developer does not sell your personal data. Data leaves the app only when needed for an action you initiate, such as sending print data to a selected printer, transferring a backup to another device, using Android's share sheet, or completing a purchase through Google Play.

5. Storage, retention, and deletion

Settings, templates, histories, logs, and other app data are stored locally until you delete them, clear the app's data, or uninstall the app. You control copies exported through backup or sharing features. Google retains purchase records according to its own policies.

6. Security

JustThermalCheck limits processing to what is needed for the features you use and relies on Android application isolation and Google Play purchase protections. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so use trusted printers and networks when sending sensitive print content.

7. Children's privacy

JustThermalCheck is a general-purpose printer utility and is not directed to children. The developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when app features or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated when changes are published.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion assistance, contact the developer through GitHub Issues or use the support contact shown on the JustThermalCheck Google Play listing.

隐私权政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 19 日

摘要：JustThermalCheck 不提供用户账号、不投放广告，也不使用统计分析。开发者不运营用于收集打印内容或个人数据的服务器。应用数据在设备本地处理和保存，除非您主动将其发送到打印机、其他设备、Android 系统分享功能，或通过 Google Play 完成购买。

1. 应用处理的信息

为了提供打印和设备管理功能，本应用可能在您的设备本地处理：

  • 打印机及连接信息，例如设备名称、蓝牙地址、IP 地址、端口和连接偏好。
  • 您创建或选择的打印内容，包括文本、指令、模板、条形码、二维码和图片。
  • 应用设置、打印任务、历史记录、日志、快捷参数及备份数据。

这些信息不会上传到开发者运营的服务器。

2. 权限及用途

  • 蓝牙和附近设备：用于发现、连接蓝牙打印机并与其通信。
  • 位置信息：在部分 Android 版本中，扫描附近蓝牙设备需要位置权限。本应用不会跟踪、保存或传输您的位置。
  • 相机和照片：仅在您主动拍摄或选择待打印图片时使用。图片在本机处理，并且只发送到您选择的打印机或目标。
  • 网络和 Wi-Fi 状态：用于局域网打印机连接、TCP 服务以及设备间数据迁移。本应用不会利用这些功能向开发者上传打印内容。

3. Google Play 购买

可选的专业版购买由 Google Play Billing 处理。Google 可能依据其隐私权政策处理 Google 账号、付款、订单和设备信息。JustThermalCheck 会查询购买状态，并接收解锁功能所需的购买令牌或签名，但不会接收或保存完整的银行卡资料。

4. 共享和披露

开发者不会出售您的个人数据。仅在您主动执行相关操作时，数据才会离开应用，例如向选定打印机发送打印数据、向另一设备迁移备份、使用 Android 系统分享功能或通过 Google Play 完成购买。

5. 保存和删除

设置、模板、历史记录、日志及其他应用数据保存在设备本地，直至您手动删除、清除应用数据或卸载应用。通过备份或分享功能导出的副本由您自行管理。Google 会依据其政策保留购买记录。

6. 安全

JustThermalCheck 将数据处理限制在您使用的功能所需范围内，并依赖 Android 应用隔离和 Google Play 购买保护。任何存储和传输方式都无法保证绝对安全，因此发送敏感打印内容时，请使用可信的打印机和网络。

7. 儿童隐私

JustThermalCheck 是通用打印工具，并非面向儿童设计。开发者不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

8. 政策变更

当应用功能或法律要求发生变化时，本政策可能更新；发布更新时，页面顶部的生效日期也会同步修改。

9. 联系方式

如有隐私问题或需要数据删除协助，请通过 GitHub Issues，或使用 JustThermalCheck Google Play 商店页面中显示的开发者支持联系方式与开发者联系。
